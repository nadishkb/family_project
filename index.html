About Us

Mirth Exports, established in 2015, is a reputed apparel manufacturing company located in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu, India – the knitwear capital of the country. We specialize in producing high-quality knitted garments that meet global fashion trends.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the premium manufacturer of knitted quality apparel in Tirupur.

Mission: To maintain a good manufacturing facility from cutting to packing in Tirupur, satisfying customers and the community's ever-changing needs.
